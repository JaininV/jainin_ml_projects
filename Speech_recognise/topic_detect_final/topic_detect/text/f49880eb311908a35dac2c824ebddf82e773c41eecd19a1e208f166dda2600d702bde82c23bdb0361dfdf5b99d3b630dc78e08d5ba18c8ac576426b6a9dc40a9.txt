tvN’s New Drama “Family” has joined the ratings race!

According to Nielsen Korea, the new drama scored 4.9% in average viewership rating nationwide, off to a promising start.

Meanwhile, KBS2’s “Oasis” garnered an average nationwide viewership rating of 6.8%, a dip from its previous week’s episode. Lastly, SBS’s “The Secret Romantic Guesthouse” recorded an average nationwide rating of 3.7%.
