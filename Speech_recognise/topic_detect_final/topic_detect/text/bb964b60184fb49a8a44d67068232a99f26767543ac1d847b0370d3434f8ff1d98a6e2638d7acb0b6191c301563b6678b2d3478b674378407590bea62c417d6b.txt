BEIJING - At the invitation of Chinese State Councilor and Foreign Minister Qin Gang, Josep Borrell, High Representative of the European Union (EU) for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and vice-president of the European Commission, will pay a visit to China and hold the 12th round of China-EU high-level strategic dialogue from April 13 to 15, Chinese foreign ministry spokesperson Wang Wenbin announced on Wednesday.
