The mysterious SSC member and a driver at a local (yet underground) maid cafe were cast for the third episode of the currently airing original TV anime Magical Destroyers, based on an original idea from designer JUN INAGAWA. The two had their voice actors announced alongside releasing their character visuals.

The Magical Destroyers original TV anime was created by designer JUN INAGAWA with Hiroshi Ikehata (FLCL Progressive) directing the series at Bibury Animation Studios. The series premiered today and is now streaming on Crunchyroll, which describes it as such:

Freedom of expression is threatened when a mysterious group, the SSC, destroy Japan’s otaku culture. However, a young revolutionary, Otaku Hero—who loves the culture more than anything—rises up. With the help of three rambunctious magical girls—Anarchy, Blue, and Pink—they’ll team up to create a world free of this oppressive rule. Be part of the rebellion to bring back fandom!
