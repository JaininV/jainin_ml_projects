An ex-parte motion filed by the governorship candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC) in Adamawa State, Senator Aisha Dahiru-Ahmed (Binani) has been declined

The Federal High Court in Abuja on Tuesday declined the request.

Binani had demanded a judicial review after she was declared winner of the Adamawa governorship election which was later annulled by INEC.

Instead, Justice Inyang Ekwo ordered her lawyer, Mohammed Sheriff, to address the court on whether it possesses the jurisdiction hear the motion relating to the happenings in Adamawa State
