In a dead rubber, ATK Mohun Bagan will square off against FC Goa in their last Group C match of Hero Super Cup 2023 on Tuesday, April 18. The match will start at 8:30 pm IST (Indian Standard Time) at EMS Corporation Stadium, Kozhikode. The ISL champions ATK Mohun Bagan have nothing to gain from this match and are likely to avail the opportunity of playing it as a preparation for the AFC Cup qualifying play-off. Sony Sports Network have the broadcasting rights of the Hero Super Cup 2023. Fans can watch the live telecast of the JFC vs GKFC match on Sony Sports 2 SD/HD channels.
