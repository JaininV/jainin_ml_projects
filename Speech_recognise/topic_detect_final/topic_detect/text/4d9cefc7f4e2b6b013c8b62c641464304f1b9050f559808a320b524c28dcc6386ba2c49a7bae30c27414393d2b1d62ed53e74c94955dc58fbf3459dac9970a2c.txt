GARNER – The Garner City Council has approved the hiring of Eric Formanek as city clerk. Formanek’s first day on the job at city hall was Monday, April 17

A Garner-Hayfield Community School alum, Formanek attended NIACC for two years, transferring to UNI. He graduated from UNI in 1999.

Following college he worked in the national marketing department of RSM McGladrey of Cedar Rapids.

In 2000 he followed one of the managing partners to his start-up company, The Whetstone Group in Marion, Iowa. He became the company’s accountant under the owner/CEO.
