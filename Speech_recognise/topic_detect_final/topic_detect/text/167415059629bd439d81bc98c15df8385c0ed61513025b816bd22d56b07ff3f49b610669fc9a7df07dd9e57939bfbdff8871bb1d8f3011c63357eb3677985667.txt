Serious concern has been raised following reports that a pet dog attacked and killed a sheep on a west Donegal beach yesterday.

It’s believed that the dog was off the lead when the incident occurred.

Gardai in Donegal have issued a statement today are reminding dog owners of the harm their pets can cause if let roam free or unsecured and not kept under control.

They are also reminding owners that its lambing season and this is a time of year where issues arise with sheep kills when dogs attack livestock.
