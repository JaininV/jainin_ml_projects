KitKat Canada offers a new twist on the iconic KitKat bar with the introduction of new KitKat Pops.

Available in classic milk and hazelnut flavours, KitKat Pops represent the latest product innovation for the brand. The poppable treats are also available in Coffee Crisp.

KitKat Pops offer the same crispy, satisfying crunch fans know and expect of the classic four-fingered KitKat bar in a new poppable form.

You can find KitKat Pops at retailers nationwide in 170g bags for grazing at home and new 70g cartons for grazing on-the-go.
