Trading activities on the floor of Nigerian Exchange Tuesday returned to a bullish run, appreciating by N5 billion.

Market capitalisation of listed equities increased by 0.02 per cent to N27.855 trillion from N27.850 trillion reported the previous day.

The NGX All Share Index also appreciated by 11.54 basis points to 51138.92 points from 51127.38 points traded the previous day.

A review of the investment showed that Transnational Corporation of Nigeria led gainers table during the day, appreciating by 9.73 per cent to close at N2.03 per unit, RTBriscoe followed with a gain of 9.09 per cent to close at N0.24 per share, International Energy Insurance added 7.50 per cent to close at N1.29 per share, Nahco gained 5.45 per cent to close at N11.60 per unit, Africa Prudential increased by 4.81 per cent to close at N5.45 per share.

On the contrary, Vitafoam Nigeria Plc topped losers chart during the day, shedding 8.65 per cent to close at N16.90 per share, Linkage Assurance trailed with a drop of 8.33 per cent to close at N0.44 per share, Chams Plc down by 8.00 per cent to close at N0.23 per unit, Sovereign Trust Insurance fell by 6.90 per cent to close at N0.27 per share while Courtvellle Business Solutions dipped by 6.67 per cent to close at N0.42 per share.

Analysis of the transactions indicated that Investors traded 1.819 billion shares valued at N5.020 billion in 4669 deals against 226.593 million shares valued at N1.567 billion in 4373 deals.

Trading in the shares of Transcorp led activity chart during the day with 1.601 billion shares valued at N3.091 billion, Living Trust followed with account 69.973 million shares worth N205.053 million, Fidelity Bank traded 15.974 million shares cost N82.335 million, Sterling Bank exchanged 13.538 million shares valued at N19.888 million, United Bank for Africa exchanged 13.096 million shares worth N102.074 million.
