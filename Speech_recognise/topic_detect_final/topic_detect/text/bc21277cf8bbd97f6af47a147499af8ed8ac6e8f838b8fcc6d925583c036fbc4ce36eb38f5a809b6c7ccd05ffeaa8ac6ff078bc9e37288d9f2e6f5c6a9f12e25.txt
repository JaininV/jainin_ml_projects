Leon’s Furniture Limited (OTCMKTS:LEFUF – Get Rating) was the recipient of a large decrease in short interest during the month of March. As of March 31st, there was short interest totalling 64,400 shares,  a decrease of 14.7% from the March 15th total of 75,500 shares. Based on an average daily volume of 200 shares, the days-to-cover ratio is currently 322.0 days.

Shares of Leon’s Furniture stock opened at $13.00 on Wednesday. Leon’s Furniture has a one year low of $11.43 and a one year high of $16.65. The firm has a fifty day moving average of $13.58 and a two-hundred day moving average of $12.88.

Separately, BMO Capital Markets upped their price target on shares of Leon’s Furniture from C$21.00 to C$22.00 in a research report on Monday, February 27th.

Leon’s Furniture Ltd. engages in the retail of home furniture, appliances, electronics and mattresses. The firm’s retail banners consists of Leon’s, The Brick, Brick Outlet and The Brick Mattress Store. It offers sofas, king beds, tables, chairs, full beds, sofa tables, pillows, bed frames, sheets, mirrors, rugs, lamps and cooktops.
