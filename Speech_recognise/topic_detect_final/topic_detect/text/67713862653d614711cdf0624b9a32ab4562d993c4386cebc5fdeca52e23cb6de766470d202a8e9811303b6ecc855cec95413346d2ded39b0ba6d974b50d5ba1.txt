Police have accused a woman of shoplifting in Kawartha Lakes.

Kawartha Lakes Police say on Monday, April 17 at approximately 7:30 a.m. while on patrol, an officer reportedly observed a female running from a William Street North business being followed by a store employee.

During the investigation police learned the female had allegedly selected a bag full of merchandise and left without paying.

A 35-year-old Lindsay woman has been charged with theft under $5,000 and possession of property obtained by crime under $5,000.

The accused will appear at the Ontario Court of Justice located at 440 Kent Street West in Lindsay on May 25.
