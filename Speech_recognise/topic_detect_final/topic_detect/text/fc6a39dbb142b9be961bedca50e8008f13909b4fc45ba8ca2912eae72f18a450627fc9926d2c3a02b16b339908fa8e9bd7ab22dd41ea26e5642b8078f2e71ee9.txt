Hong Kong stocks opened marginally lower Wednesday following a tepid lead from Wall Street that came on the back of lukewarm bank earnings figures. The Hang Seng Index fell 0.08 percent, or 17.51 points, to 20,633.00. The Shanghai Composite Index eased 0.06 percent, or 1.98 points, to 3,391.35, while the Shenzhen Composite Index on China's second exchange lost 0.13 percent, or 2.83 points, to 2,135.97.
