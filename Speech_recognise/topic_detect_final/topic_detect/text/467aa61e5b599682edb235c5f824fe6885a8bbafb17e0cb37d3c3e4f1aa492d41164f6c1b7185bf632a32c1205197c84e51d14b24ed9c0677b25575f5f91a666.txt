YOUNGSTOWN, Ohio (News Talk 1480 WHBC) – A Youngstown Catholic diocesan priest will be working at the main office soon, the Vatican.

Father Shawn Conoboy who also served as an adjunct professor at Walsh University has accepted an appointment with Rome’s assembly for Promoting Integral Human Development.

He’s currently a pastor at several Portage County parishes.

He will work with Bishops Conferences around the country, helping identify the needs of individual churches.
