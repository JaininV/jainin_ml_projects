Tailored bases have flat parallel faces that function as ZOCALADOS models allowing precision OCLUSAL according to the original occlusal register of each person.

1) Preparation of 3D models.

2) Transfer the 3D model to the meshmixer or other program.

3) Load the base move to the model (you can decrease or increase the size to be proportional to the model).

4) Match the base with the model and export.

5) Ready to print.
