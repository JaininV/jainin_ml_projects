How does this work?

Burlington has released the registration schedule for a number of summer recreation programs.

Swimming lessons and aquatic leadership programs will be open for registration on April 22 at 9 a.m.

Most other adult programs will be open for registration on May 27 at 9 a.m.

As well as the swimming and other aquatic programs, some of the programs available this year include skating and pickleball as well as summer camps, student theatre programs and music.

The City of Burlington also offers family programs and a large number of outdoor play activities along with those at community centres.

Volunteer opportunities are also available to assist or lead many of these programs.
