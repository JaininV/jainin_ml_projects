Three people got out safely when fire broke out Monday afternoon at a Brentwood Bay home on Peggy Anne Crescent, off Hagan Road.

The Central Saanich Fire Department responded about 3:30 p.m. with 17 firefighters and mutual-aid assistance from North Saanich and Sidney crews.

The homeowner initially tried to douse the blaze with a fire extinguisher.

Emergency Social Services was contacted to help the displaced family with accommodation.

Cause has not yet been determined.
