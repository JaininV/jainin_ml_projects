iQOO recently celebrated its three-year anniversary in India. As part of the celebration, iQOO announced the third anniversary sale, bringing big discounts to several iQOO smartphones, including its flagship iQOO 11 5G.

The iQOO 11 5G with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage is available at a discounted price of Rs 54,990 on Amazon India as part of the third anniversary sale. Additionally, the iQOO 11 5G price in India is set at Rs 59,990 for the 16GB/256GB variant.

Both iQOO 11 5G models are available at a discounted price of Rs 5,000 on Amazon India and iQOO's E-store. However, customers can also avail a discount of Rs 5,000 on ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank credit and debit cards and EMI transactions on both the website and Amazon.

This would bring the effective price of the iQOO 11 5G down to Rs 49,990 on Amazon India and Rs 49,999 on the official e-store. Thus, making it the most affordable Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 smartphone in India to date. Additionally, iQOO is also offering a special gift box for the first 1,000 customers who order the iQOO 11 5G from its official website.

Apart from its 2023 flagships, iQOO is also offering discounts on its 9 series from last year. The iQOO 9 price in India is now set at Rs 35,990 and Rs 40,990 for the 8GB/128GB and 12GB/256GB variants, respectively. The iQOO 9 Pro price in India is set at Rs 44,990 and Rs 49,990 for the 8GB/256GB and 12GB/256GB models, respectively.

Customers who purchase the iQOO 9 and iQOO 9 Pro on Amazon or the iQOO E-store will be entitled to an extra Rs 5,000 off when using ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank credit and debit cards and EMI transactions. iQOO is also offering a special gift box for the first 1,000 website customers.
