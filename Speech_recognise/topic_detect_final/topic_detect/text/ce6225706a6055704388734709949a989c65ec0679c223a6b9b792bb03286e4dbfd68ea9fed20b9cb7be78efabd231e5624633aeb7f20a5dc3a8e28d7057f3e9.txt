A new kid on the block Toby Davyd is out with a brand new single titled “Heart Cry“.

Toby Davyd is a worship leader, recording artiste, and music minister. He is a deep worshipper who worships from the depth of his heart.

In this new song titled ” Heart Cry” Toby Davyd delivered a heart-felt worship. His heart cry could definitely be felt in this beautiful song.

Heart cry as the title implies is a cry for more of God, it is a cry for a continual longing and reaching out to God. It is a call to worship.
