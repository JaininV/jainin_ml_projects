Culture plays a vital role in shaping the society we live in. It defines the way people live, interact, and communicate with each other. Culture encompasses a broad range of factors, including language, customs, values, beliefs, and traditions that have been passed down from generation to generation.

Moreover, culture plays a crucial role in preserving and transmitting knowledge, skills, and traditions from one generation to the next. It provides a framework for understanding history, literature, and the arts, helping to ensure that valuable cultural artifacts and traditions are not lost or forgotten over time.
