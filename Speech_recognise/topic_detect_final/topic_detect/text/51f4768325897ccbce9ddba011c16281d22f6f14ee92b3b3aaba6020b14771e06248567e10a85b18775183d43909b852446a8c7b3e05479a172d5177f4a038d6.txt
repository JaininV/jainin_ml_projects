The investigation into a horrific helicopter crash in the streets of Broome that killed a 12-year-old girl and the pilot found the local tourism stalwart had a “high-risk appetite” and a willingness to expose others to it.

The horrific tragedy unfolded at an industrial property in Bilingurr, in the Kimberley town’s northern suburbs, on July 4, 2020, fatally injuring Mandurah girl Amber Jess Millar and Troy Thomas, 40, the former owner of Horizontal Falls Seaplane Adventures.
