Due to insurgency attacks in the Far North Region of Cameroon, persons started fleeing Cameroon to Adamawa State in June 2022. Currently there are 22,200 individuals seeking refuge in Adamawa State, Local Government Areas (LGAs) of Madagali, Michika, Mubi North, and Mubi South. Most of the refugees are from Mutas, Wupai, Ndrok, KulKubai and Dulum villages in the Far North Region of Cameroon.
