Salman Khan’s highly awaited action entertainer ‘Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan’ will be released in theatres on April 21. Now, just a few days before the film’s premiere, the makers have released a new BTS video of the film’s action scenes in which Salman Khan can be seen doing acrobatic perilous feats on his own without the assistance of a body double.

The 1 minute 20 second video delves further into the creation of the film’s spectacular action set pieces. The video opens with Salman and stunt director discussing about choreography of the action set piece.  In the background, we can also see a large set. Then, in the train action set piece, we witness Salman performing his own acrobatics stunts.

The video also suggests that Salman was involved in the choreography of these action set pieces, as we see him communicate and exchange ideas with the action choreographer. There is also a brief shot of Salman beating up several masked stuntmen.

The new BTS video also shows Salman resurrecting his ‘action hero’ image for his fans by performing some high-octane action stunts. With the release of this behind-the-scenes action footage, fans’ anticipation for this long-awaited action entertainment has only intensified.

Just yesterday, ‘Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan’ makers had dropped the film’s new song ‘O Balle Balle’ on YouTube. The song has already become a huge hit and has got more than 4.5 million views on YouTube within 24 hours.
