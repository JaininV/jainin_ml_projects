Ke Huy Quan has had a Hollywood comeback that is truly unparalleled. He began his career as a child actor by starring in two back-to-back hit movies, The Goonies and Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom. But, soon after his career took off, it slowly began to fade, until a few decades later when he got a starring role in Everything Everywhere All at Once.
