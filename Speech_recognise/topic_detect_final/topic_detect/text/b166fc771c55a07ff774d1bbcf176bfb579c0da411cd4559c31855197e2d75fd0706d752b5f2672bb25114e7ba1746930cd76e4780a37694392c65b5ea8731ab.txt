Cranberry Township is asking for bids to accomplish an upcoming project.

Township Supervisors are soliciting for a company to provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision required for the Fox Run Sanitary Sewer Bypass and Storm Sewer Improvements Project.

This project will include the installation of a bypass sewer, and the replacement of the existing storm sewer piping along Fox Run Road with more details available upon request.

Cranberry Township uses the electronic bid management platform PennBid to provide additional information and collect contract documents.
