Chelsea co-owner, Todd Boehly, has predicted the scoreline of tonight’s Champions League first-leg clash between Real Madrid and the Blues.

Real Madrid will on Wednesday night host Chelsea in the Champions League last-8 tie at the Santiago Bernabeu.

Chelsea heads to the game after losing 1-0 against Wolves in their last fixture in the Premier League while Real Madrid lost 3-2 against Villarreal in La Liga.

Speaking to Sky Sports News, Boehly said: “Chelsea’s going to win [Real Madrid] 3-0.”

The American added, “I would say have a lot of faith. We are going to win 3-0 tonight,” he reiterated.

The kick-off time for the match is 8 pm.
