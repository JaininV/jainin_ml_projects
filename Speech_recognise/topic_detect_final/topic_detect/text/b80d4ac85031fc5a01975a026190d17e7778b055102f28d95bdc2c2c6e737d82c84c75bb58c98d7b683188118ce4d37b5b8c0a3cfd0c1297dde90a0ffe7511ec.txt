LINCOLN, Ala. – The 2023 edition of the Talladega County Special Olympics will be held this Thursday, April 20, at Lincoln High School’s (LHS) Keith Howard Memorial Stadium.

Check-in will begin at 9:00 a.m. and last one hour until the festivities get underway at 10:00 a.m.

The Special Olympics Parade will commence which will be quickly followed by the introduction of participants, the national anthem played by the LHS marching band, welcoming remarks, and the lighting of the Olympic Torch.
