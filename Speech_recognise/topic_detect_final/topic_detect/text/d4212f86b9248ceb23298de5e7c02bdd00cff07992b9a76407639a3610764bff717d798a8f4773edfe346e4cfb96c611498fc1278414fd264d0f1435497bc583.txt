About Cambridge

The areas, neighborhoods and squares of Cambridge

City of Cambridge offices, libraries and senior centers will be closed Monday on the Patriots’ Day holiday, and payments will not be required at city parking meters and parking meter pay stations. The offices at Cambridge Cemetery, 76 Coolidge Ave., West Cambridge, will be closed for the holiday, but the gates will be open from 7 a.m. to 7:30 p.m.

Curbside collection of trash, recycling, compost and yard waste will be suspended on the holiday and be one day behind for the rest of the week. There will be no street cleaning on the holiday.

CCTV is closed Monday in observance as well.

Please consider making a financial contribution to maintain, expand and improve Cambridge Day.
