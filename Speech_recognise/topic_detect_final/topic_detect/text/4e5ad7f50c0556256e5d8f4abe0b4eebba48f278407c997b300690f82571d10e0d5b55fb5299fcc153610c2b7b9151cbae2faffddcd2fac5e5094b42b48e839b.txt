Lee Seung Gi and Lee Da In denied having product placement at their wedding.

The celebrity couple officially tied the knot on April 7 at a hotel in Seoul, and suspicions that there was product placement at their wedding arose online. In a video of the wedding, netizens are saying you can see projected images on a screen behind Lee Seung Gi and Lee Da In, and while he was serenading her, the jewelry brand name for the tiara Lee Da In wore at the wedding showed on screen.

On April 12, Lee Seung Gi's representative told media outlets, "It's not product placement. It's a video produced by Lee Seung Gi for his serenade at the wedding. It was put in as an insert scene before the couple came out just to look pretty."

In other news, Lee Seung Gi and Lee Da In were recently spotted shopping together in Singapore.
