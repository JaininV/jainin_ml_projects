Former Karnataka chief minister BS Yediyurappa on Wednesday exuded confidence in his son Vijayendra winning with a margin of more than 50,000 votes in the May 10 state assembly election. "Everybody insisted that my son contest should contest from Shikaripura because I'm not contesting. Vijayendra will win with a margin of over 50,000 votes. He will get complete support from the entire community," he said.
