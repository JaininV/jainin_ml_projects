Fans and followers of actors Vic Sotto, Jose Manalo and Maja Salvador has something to look forward to after Maja announced that they will be having a new sitcom.

Titled “Open 24/7”, the said sitcom is produced by MZet Productions and will be aired on GMA7.

On Instagram, Maja shared some videos of their pictorial with the following caption:

panibagong pangarap ko na naman ang natupad! 🙏 last January nung malaman ko ang balita tungkol sa sitcom… finally Ito na at tuloy na tuloy na! A million thanks Bossing sa tiwala”
