Two men have been arrested after police executed a number of warrants.

On Wednesday morning, officers were in the Dudley area of North Tyneside in connected with an ongoing probe.

They attended four properties on Green Crescent and Green Lane and made two arrests.

Two men, aged 26 and 32, were arrested on suspicion of affray. They remain in police custody.

A Northumbria Police spokesperson said: "Officers have been in the Dudley area of North Tyneside this morning, executing a number of warrants in connection with an ongoing investigation.

"As part of their enquiries officers attended four addresses on Green Crescent and Green Lane and arrested two men, aged 26 and 32 on suspicion of affray.

"The men in remain police custody at this time."
