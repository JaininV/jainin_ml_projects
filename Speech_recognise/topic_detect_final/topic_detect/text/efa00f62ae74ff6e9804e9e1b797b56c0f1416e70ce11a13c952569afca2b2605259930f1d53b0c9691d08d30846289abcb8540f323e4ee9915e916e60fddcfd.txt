A lecturer with one of the Universities in the South East(name withheld) reportedly escaped through the backdoor after his student stormed lecture hall putting on palmpay polo.

According to one of the students who spoke with our correspondent on the condition of anonymity, they were surprised how the lecturer “disappeared” after spotting the student wearing the polo.

“We were shocked how our lecturer disappeared within a twinkle of an eye immediately he spotted the student coming. We don’t know his reasons as the lecturer never returned back, the student told our correspondent”.

Palmpay, one of the leading online banks in Nigeria has continuously denied arresting customers who default their loan payments.

Recently, they warned that those who are parading fake pictures online to stop or they will taken to court.
