Samsung Galaxy S22 comes with a 6.1-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display and a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels. The display supports HDR10+ and up to 120Hz refresh rate.

The smartphone is equipped with a Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 paired with 8GB RAM and up to 256GB storage. It comes with Android 12 OS out of the box but can be upgradable to Android 13.

It boasts a triple camera setup on the back holding a 50-megapixel primary camera, a 10-megapixel telephoto sensor and a 12-megapixel ultra-wide lens. On the front, it sports a 10-megapixel selfie shooter.

The Samsung Galaxy S22 is packed with a 3700mAh battery with 25-watt wired fast charging and 15-watt wireless.
