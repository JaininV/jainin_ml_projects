He is not expected to face serious competition for the nomination of his Democratic Party, but his approval levels remain low by historical standards.  Donald Trump, who is also running for president in 2024, spent much of his 2017-2021 presidency with similarly low approval levels, bottoming out at 33% in December 2017. Previous presidents only occasionally experienced approval levels that low.
