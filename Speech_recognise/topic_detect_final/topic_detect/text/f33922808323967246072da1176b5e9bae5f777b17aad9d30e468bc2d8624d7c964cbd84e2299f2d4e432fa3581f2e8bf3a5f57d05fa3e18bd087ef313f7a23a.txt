,  /PRNewswire/ -- Pomerantz LLP is investigating claims on behalf of investors of  Tilray Brands, Inc. ("Tilray" or the "Company") (NASDAQ: TLRY). Such investors are advised to contact Robert S. Willoughby at  [email protected] or 888-476-6529, ext. 7980.

The investigation concerns whether Tilray and certain of its officers and/or directors have engaged in securities fraud or other unlawful business practices.

On April 10, 2023, Tilray issued a press release reporting financial results for its fiscal third quarter ended February 28, 2023. Among other items, Tilray reported that its major segments missed revenue forecasts, while the Company reported a gross loss of $11.7 million for the quarter, compared to a gross profit of $40 million for the same period in the prior year.

On this news, Tilray's stock price fell $0.22 per share, or 8.03%, to close at $2.52 per share on April 11, 2023.
