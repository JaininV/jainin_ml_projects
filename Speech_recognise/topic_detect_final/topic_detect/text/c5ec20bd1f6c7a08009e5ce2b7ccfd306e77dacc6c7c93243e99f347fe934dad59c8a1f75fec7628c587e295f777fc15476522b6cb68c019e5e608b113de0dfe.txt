FAIRFIELD – Scholarship applications are being accepted by the Tri-City NAACP for graduating seniors who attend high schools in Fairfield-Suisun or Vacaville.

Requirements include a minimum 2.5 grade-point average, based on a 4.0 scale, and the applicant must be planning to attend a community college, university, vocational or technical school.

The scholarship application requires a completed application, a current official transcript, two letters of recommendation, and a 500-word essay.

A headshot must accompany the application only for publicity on the branch’s website.

For more information, and to download the application, go to tri-citynaacp.org/tri-city-scholarship-1. The deadline is May 31.
