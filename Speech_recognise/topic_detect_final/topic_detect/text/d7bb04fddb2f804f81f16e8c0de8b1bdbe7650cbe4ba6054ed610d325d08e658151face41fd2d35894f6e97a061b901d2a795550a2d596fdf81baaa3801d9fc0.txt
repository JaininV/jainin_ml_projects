Volkswagen India has planned to launch six new avatars of the Taigun and Virtus in 2023. At the company's annual brand conference, the Taigun GT Plus Black, Taigun GT Sport, Virtus GT Plus Manual, Virtus GT Plus Black, Taigun GT Matte and Taigun GT Trail were unveiled. We took a look at all six vehicles and all the changes and updates they bring along with them. According to Volkswagen India, these new additions have been brought after customer feedback. The company also said that these new models will launch at the start of June 2023.
