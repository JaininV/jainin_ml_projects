Crawfish lovers and country music enthusiasts, here’s your reminder that the Mississippi Coast Coliseum Crawfish Music Festival begins tomorrow.

The crew at the coliseum finished setting up this evening. Gates will be open for the public on April 19th and 20th from 5 p.m. to 10 p.m., April 21st from 5 p.m. to 11 p.m., April 22nd from 1 p.m. to 11 p.m. and April 23rd 1 p.m. to 8 p.m.

Mississippi Coast Coliseum Executive Director Matt McDonnell says this combination of food, music, and rides makes this event an absolute must for any coastal resident. “The rides are great, the food’s great. A lot of good environment here. It’s safe, it’s easy to get your strollers if you have young kids to move around. A lot of kids rides that are going to be set up as well. If you want to have a great Saturday afternoon, Sunday afternoon into the evening, this is the place.”

Tickets can be found at the gate or on ticketmaster.com. Tickets cost $10 for Wednesday and Thursday and $25 for Friday through Sunday.

Children under six get in for free.
