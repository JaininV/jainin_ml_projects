It is Spring, which means the home improvement Travelers are out. We had a report of the paving scammers in Whitmire. The offer to pave or seal your driveway and will even give a guarantee. The material is substandard if not completely fake. The generally drive newer model trucks and have a spraying machine in the back similar to a power washer. One of the suspects was a dark skinned white male with jet black hair. If someone comes to your home offering to do home improvements call 911.
