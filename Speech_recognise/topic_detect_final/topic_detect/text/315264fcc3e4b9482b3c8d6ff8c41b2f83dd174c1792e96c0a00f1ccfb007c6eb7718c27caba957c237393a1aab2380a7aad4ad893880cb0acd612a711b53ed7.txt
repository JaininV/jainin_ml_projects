Auburn Tennis Day is returning to the city next month.

Taking place at the Y-Field, three free one hour sessions will be offered for beginner, intermediate, and advanced players.

This event is a collaboration between Auburn’s Parks & Rec Department, Auburn Community Tennis, FLX Tennis, Champions for Life, and the US Tennis Association’s Eastern Section.

You can register for a session on the USTA’s website.
