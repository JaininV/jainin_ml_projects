One of my favorite recent weddings took place in a stunning backyard in Paradise Valley, Arizona. The colors, the joy, the intimate setting…it was pure BLISS, pun intended! Large weddings are fun,  but photographing the small, intimate ones can be pure joy. It’s calmer, quieter and you really get to experience all the emotions.
