How To Treat Your Cervicogenic Headache With Chiropractic?

Cervicogenic headaches are sometimes mistaken for tension and migraine headaches, both of which can result in neck pain. This type of headache is a secondary headache. An underlying problem, such as a neck injury, an infection, or extremely high blood pressure, might cause secondary headaches. They differ from primary headaches like migraine and cluster headaches because of this.
