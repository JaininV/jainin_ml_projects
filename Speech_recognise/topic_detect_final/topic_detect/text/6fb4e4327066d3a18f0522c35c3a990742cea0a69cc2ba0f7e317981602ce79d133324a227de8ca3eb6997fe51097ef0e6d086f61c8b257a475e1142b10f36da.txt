ISLAMABAD, (UrduPoint / Pakistan Point News - 19th Apr, 2023 ) :Minister for Commerce Syed Naveed Qamar called on Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif here on Wednesday and presented him with a donation cheque of Rs 8 million for the PM's Relief Fund for Earthquake Victims of Turkiye and Syria.

The minister presented the donation cheque on behalf of the Sialkot Chamber of Commerce and Industries.
