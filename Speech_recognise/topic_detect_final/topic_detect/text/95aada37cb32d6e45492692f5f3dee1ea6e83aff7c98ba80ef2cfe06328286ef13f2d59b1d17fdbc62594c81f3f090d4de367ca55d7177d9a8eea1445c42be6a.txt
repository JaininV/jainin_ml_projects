A Melancthon man has been charged in relation to an investigation into stolen property in Grey Highlands.

Grey Bruce OPP recovered numerous stolen items at a property on Grey Road 4 on Jan. 24.

On April 9, at about 3:30 p.m., police arrested a 36-year-old man and charged him with possession of property obtained by crime over $5,000.

The accused was released and will appear at a later date in Owen Sound court.
