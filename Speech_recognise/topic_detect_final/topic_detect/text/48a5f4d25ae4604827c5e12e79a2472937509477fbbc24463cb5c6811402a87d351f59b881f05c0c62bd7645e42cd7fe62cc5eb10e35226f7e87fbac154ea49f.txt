DJ Cuppy reveals plan to become a backup singer for Asake

Florence Otedola, a Nigerian musician and billionaire’s daughter who is popularly known as DJ Cuppy, has disclosed her future plans for her music career.

Cuppy has expressed her desire to become a backup singer for her colleague, Ahmed Ololade, who is well known as Asake.

In a post on her Instagram story, she enquired about the process of becoming one of Asake’s backup singers.

Cuppy asked her followers to provide her with information on how to achieve her goal by writing:

“Plix can someone tell me how I can become one of the backup singers for @asakemusic ?”
