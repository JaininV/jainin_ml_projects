The upcoming NWA Crockett Cup will feature Tyrus, as well as Kerry & Ricky Morton as revealed on this week’s edition of NWA Powerrr.

Reigning NWA Worlds Heavyweight Champion Tyrus announced on the show that he will be entering the tag team tournament, which takes place on June 3rd and 4th. Jordan Clearwater pitched himself as Tyrus’ partner in the tournament, but the champion noted that he is yet to decide who he will team with.

The Mortons later announced their participation in the tournament.
