The most watermelons smashed in one minute is 70, and was achieved by Ronald Sarchian (USA) in Van Nuys, California, USA, on 30 August 2020.

Ron holds multiple record titles including fastest time to break open five piñatas and most cornhole bags stacked in 30 seconds.
