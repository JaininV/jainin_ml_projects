Miss Rodriguez, who has two kids for the superstar footballer, said the former Real Madrid and Juventus highest earner refused to buy her a bag despite her request.

The reason behind the former Manchester United’s denial was that he believed his girlfriend already had too many bags.

The Argentine model has previously shown that she possesses around 150 bags.

She told El Hormiguero, “I asked for the same one I have but bigger. He told me, ‘I refuse. I’m not going to buy it for you. I tell him, ‘Well, I’ll buy it myself.'”

Ronaldo’s partner has been providing a unique insight into her life in the latest season of her Netflix series, ‘I am Georgina’.
