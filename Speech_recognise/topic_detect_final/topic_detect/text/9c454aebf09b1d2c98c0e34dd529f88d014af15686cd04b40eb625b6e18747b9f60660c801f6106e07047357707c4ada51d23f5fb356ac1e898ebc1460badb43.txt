OPP have arrested a 62-year-old Peterborough resident for allegedly driving while impaired.

A member of the Peterborough County OPP conducted a traffic stop on a motor vehicle in Peterborough on April 15, at approximately 4:20 p.m., say police.

The officer spoke with the driver and allegedly found them to be under the influence of alcohol.
