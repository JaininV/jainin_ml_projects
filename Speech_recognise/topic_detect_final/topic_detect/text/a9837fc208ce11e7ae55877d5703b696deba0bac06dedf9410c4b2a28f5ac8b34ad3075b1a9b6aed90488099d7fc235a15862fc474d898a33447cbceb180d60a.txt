ISLAMABAD: Interior Minister Rana Sana Ullah has said Lahore High Court (LHC) has prevented from harassing Imran Khan in illegal way and not in legal way.

He said this while talking to a private TV channel here Wednesday.

He advised Imran Khan to celebrate Eid without any worries. Federal government has no intention to conduct any operation against Imran Khan.

Citing to Supreme Court (SC) decision with reference to polls in Punjab he said lobbying is seen behind the appointment of any judge. Due to this lobbying division of 8-7 is being seen in SC today.
