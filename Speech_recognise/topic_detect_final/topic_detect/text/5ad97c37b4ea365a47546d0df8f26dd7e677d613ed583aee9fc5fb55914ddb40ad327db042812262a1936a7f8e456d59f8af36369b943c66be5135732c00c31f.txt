Amid the ongoing preparations, Tamil Nadu’s Madurai is all hyped-up for the upcoming Chithirai festival. The annual festival just got its gold-plated beauty with the festival being so close. Lord Kallazhagar’s gold-plated idol of a horse, revered as ‘Vahana’, is all set to mesmerize the devotees with its beauty. Along with the ‘Vahana’, work is also being done on other idols for the festival. The much-celebrated festival marks the wedding of Lord Sundareswara and Goddess Meenakshi.
