Assuming she had time to read them, the Sunday newspapers made pleasant reading for Sinn Fein President, and Dublin TD, Mary Lou McDonald.

These publications revealed, via an opinion poll, that Sinn Fein remains the most popular political party in the Republic of Ireland. Her critics will say, ‘popular for doing very little’. Regardless, it’s all good news for this all-island organisation.
