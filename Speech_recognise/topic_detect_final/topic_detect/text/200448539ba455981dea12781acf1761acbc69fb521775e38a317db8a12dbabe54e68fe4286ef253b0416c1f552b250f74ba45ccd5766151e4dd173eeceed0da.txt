The Argentinians love their empanadas. These pastries come in a huge variety of flavors. Here’s a recipe for empanadas stuffed with beef for four people.

430g flour salt

100g butter

100ml water

2 pcs onions

4 pcs spring onions

paprika powder

pepper

chili

430g rumpsteak

1 egg, hard-boiled

50g green olives

Bake in the oven at 200-220ºC for 10 minutes.
