The station 42 and third rock crews respond to a deadly explosion at an abandoned mine. Meanwhile, Bode faces a difficult decision that could have serious consequences.

This episode is written by Julia Fontana & Barbara Kaye Friend and directed by Joy Lane.

In Fire Country, Bode Donovan, a young convict seeking redemption and a shortened prison sentence by joining an unconventional prison release firefighting program in Northern California, where he and other inmates are partnered with elite firefighters to extinguish massive, unpredictable wildfires across the region.

Fire Country is produced by CBS Studios. Jerry Bruckheimer, Jonathan Littman and KristieAnne Reed are executive producers.
