According to bigcountryhomepage.com, a fugitive who on Friday fled from police in Cross Plains after the burglary of a building was apprehended Saturday in Brownwood.

Justin Parker was arrested around 1 p.m. Saturday after being caught with a stolen vehicle, the report states. A location in Brownwood where the arrest occurred was not provided.

Parker is currently charged with a felony and a misdemeanor with more changes potentially pending.

Further information will be provided as it become available.
