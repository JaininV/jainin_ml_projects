CHARLESTON, SC—Butler Snow LLP is pleased to announce that R. Nicholas Felix (Nick) has joined the firm’s Commercial Litigation practice group in the Charleston, South Carolina, office effective April 1, 2023. He represents individual and corporate clients in complex commercial and catastrophic injury matters. He is also a licensed and certified State and Federal arbitrator and mediator, with a substantial portion of his practice being devoted to Alternative Dispute Resolution.

Felix joins Butler Snow with more than thirty years of legal experience and an extensive professional portfolio. His commercial litigation practice includes contract, commercial and residential construction, trust and estates, employment, director and officer liability, business torts, and financial services cases.
