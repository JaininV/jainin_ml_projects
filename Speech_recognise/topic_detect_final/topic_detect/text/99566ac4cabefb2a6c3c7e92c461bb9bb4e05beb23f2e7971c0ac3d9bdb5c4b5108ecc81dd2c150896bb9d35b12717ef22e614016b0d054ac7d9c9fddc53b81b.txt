What are you looking for?

The HR Associate will provide strong support to the Human Resources team across various functions such as but not limited to Recruitment, Employee Engagement, Benefits, Payroll and other HR support. This includes coordinating and scheduling interviews, managing Notice of Personnel Action (NPA) and Letter of Appointment (LOA) issuance, assisting offboarding process, distributing gov't forms (as needed), and assisting the HR unit to fulfil a variety of necessary HR tasks.

At least 1 year of work experience with same capacity or background

Capacity to collaborate efficiently with others, adopting a team-oriented approach.

Strong proficiency in multi-tasking and handling multiple priorities with a high sense of urgency

Understands and believes in Save the Children's mission and vision; commits to its objectives and willing to adopt Save the Children's methodology and framework.
