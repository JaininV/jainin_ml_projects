It’s time to unshackle the magic. The Mageseeker: A League of Legends Story™ is out now. From Digital Sun, creators of Moonlighter, comes The Mageseeker: A League of Legends Story™. In this indie 2D pixel action RPG set in the League of Legends universe, play as Sylas, a spell-stealing mage now freed from his captivity at the hands of the oppressive Mageseekers. Wield the chains that once bound you and bring Demacia to its reckoning.
