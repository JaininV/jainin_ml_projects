All Things Go Festival announces 2023 lineup with boygenius, Lana Del Rey, Maggie Rogers, and more.

The festival returns to the Merriweather Post Pavilion in Columbia, Maryland from September 30–October 1.

(L) boygenius. Photo by Matt Grub (M) Lana Del Rey. Photo via publicist (R) Maggie Rogers. Photo by Zoe Ghertner

The 2023 lineup for All Things Go Festival 2023 has arrived with boygenius, Lana Del Rey and Maggie Rogers headlining the two-day event this fall at the Merriweather Post Pavilion in Columbia, Maryland. Taking place from September 30–October 1, All Things Go will also include performances from Carly Rae Jepsen, Sudan Archives, Alex G, Ethel Cain, Beabadoobee, Arlo Parks, and many other FADER-approved acts. A fan presale for tickets begins this Thursday, April 20 at 10 a.m. EDT with a general sale beginning Friday, April 21 at 10 a.m. EDT. Visit the festival’s website for more information, and check out the full lineup below.
