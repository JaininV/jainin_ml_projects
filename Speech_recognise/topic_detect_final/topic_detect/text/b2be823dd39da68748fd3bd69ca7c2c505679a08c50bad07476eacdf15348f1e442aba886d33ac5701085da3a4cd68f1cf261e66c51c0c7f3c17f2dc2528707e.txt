Hamster is bringing Dig Dug II to Switch as its latest Arcade Archives game, the company has announced. A worldwide release is planned for April 13, 2023.

Here’s a brief overview of the title:

Dig Dug II is an action arcade video game developed and published in Japan by Namco in 1985, and is a sequel to 1982’s Dig Dug. Pookas and fire-breathing Fygars return as the enemies, but the side view tunneling of the original is replaced with an overhead view of an island maze.

Arcade Archives Dig Dug II will cost $7.99 / €6.99 / £6.29 on the Switch eShop.
