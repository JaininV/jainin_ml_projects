A new Book Club: The Next Chapter trailer has been released, previewing the next adventure for the titular quartet of friends. The trailer shows the group decide to jet to Italy for a bachelorette party, where all sorts of adventures seem to take place. The sequel is set to hit theaters on May 12, 2023.

“The highly anticipated sequel follows our four best friends as they take their book club to Italy for the fun girls trip they never had,” reads the film’s synopsis. “When things go off the rails and secrets are revealed, their relaxing vacation turns into a once-in-a-lifetime cross-country adventure.”

Book Club: The Next Chapter was directed by Bill Holderman and written by Holderman and Erin Simms. It stars Diane Keaton, Jane Fonda, Candice Bergen, Mary Steenburgen, and Craig T. Nelson, with Giancarlo Giannini, Andy Garcia, and Don Johnson.
