It turns out that a review on the travel app TripAdvisor can be one of the best things, or one of the worst, that can happen to a facility.

Recently, we saw the downside with the worst 'tourist traps' in South Dakota, Iowa, and Minnesota based on low TripAdvisor ratings.

Now, Stacker is using stellar TripAdvisor reviews to rank the top museums in South Dakota, Iowa, and Minnesota.

It has an average rating of 4.5 (out of 5) based on 1,758 reviews.

In Iowa, the top-rated museum sits on the banks of the state's busiest waterway.

Thy National Mississippi River Museum & Aquarium in Dubuque is part aquarium, part museum, and part science center, It spans 14 acres and is home to over 100 species of animals and thousands of historic artifacts.

The best museum in Minnesota is the Minneapolis Institute of Art. It is home to more than 90,000 works of art representing 5,000 years of world history.

It scored a perfect 5 out of 5 rating, based on 2,388 reviews.
