SIMFEROPOL (UrduPoint News / Sputnik - 19th April, 2023) One person was killed and two more were injured as a result of Ukraine's mortar shelling of the city of Nova Kakhovka in the Kherson region, the city administration said on Wednesday.

"As a result of the morning shelling of Nova Kakhovka on April 19 ... (Ukrainian military) killed one person and two received mine-explosive injuries," the administration said in a statement.
