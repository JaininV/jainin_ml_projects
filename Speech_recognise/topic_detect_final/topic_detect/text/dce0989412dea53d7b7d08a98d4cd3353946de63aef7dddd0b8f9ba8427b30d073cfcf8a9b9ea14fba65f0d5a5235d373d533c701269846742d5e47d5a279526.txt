The Anup Singh directorial takes place in the Thar desert and stars Khan as a camel dealer who falls in love with Nooran, a woman who is the only one who knows the song with which to treat a Scorpion bite.

The Song of the Scorpions also stars Tillotama Shome, Golshifteh Farahani, Waheeda Rehman, and Shashank Arora. Khan passed away at the age of 54 after battling a rare form of cancer.
