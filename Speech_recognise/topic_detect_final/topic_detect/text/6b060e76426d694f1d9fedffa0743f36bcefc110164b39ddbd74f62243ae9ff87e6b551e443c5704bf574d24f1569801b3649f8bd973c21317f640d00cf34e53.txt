DALLAS, TX—Trial lawyers Ted Riney, Marcos Ronquillo and Andrew Soule have joined forces to form Riney Ronquillo Soule, a Dallas-based firm committed to leveraging talent and experience to deliver maximized results and business-focused solutions to complex litigation.

Mssrs. Riney, Ronquillo and Soule bring unique perspectives forged by decades of experience delivering trusted counsel and premium services to a range of clients, including Fortune 500 companies, businesses of all sizes, governmental organizations and public entities.

In addition to complex commercial litigation in state and federal venues, Mr. Ronquillo’s diverse practice includes international arbitration, due diligence, financial transactions, cross-border representations in Mexico and Latin America, and representation before governmental and regulatory agencies. A recognized business leader with a commitment to civic engagement, he is considered one of the most accomplished trial attorneys in Texas.
