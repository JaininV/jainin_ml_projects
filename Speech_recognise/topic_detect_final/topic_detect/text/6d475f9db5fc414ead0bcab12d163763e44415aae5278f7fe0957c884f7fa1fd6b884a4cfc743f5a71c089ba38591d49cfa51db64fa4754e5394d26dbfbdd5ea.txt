By Breaking Belize News Staff: A Ladyville man is out on bail after being accused of attacking a woman on two separate occasions.

Thirty-three-year-old Elward McKay appeared unrepresented in the Belize City Magistrate’s Court charged with two counts of wounding upon a woman. One incident allegedly occurred on March 29 and the other allegedly occurred on March 31.

He pleaded not guilty to both counts and was granted bail in the sum of $1,000 plus one surety of the same which he met.

He is due back in court on June 13.
