Long Island is jam packed with entertainment options for locals and visitors. Whether you’re looking to  visit a museum , ride go-karts, or catch a movie at the theater, there is something here for you. Long Island even has extreme entertainment options for those who are looking to get their adrenaline pumping by trying something more on the wild side.

Extreme entertainment and extreme sports are not for the faint of heart. Often these activities require traveling at fast speeds, reaching increasing heights, a high level of physical exertion, and highly technical and specialized equipment needed to perform the activity. If these are all things that appeal to you then it might be time to take recreation to a whole new level and try your luck at one of these extreme entertainment facilities.
