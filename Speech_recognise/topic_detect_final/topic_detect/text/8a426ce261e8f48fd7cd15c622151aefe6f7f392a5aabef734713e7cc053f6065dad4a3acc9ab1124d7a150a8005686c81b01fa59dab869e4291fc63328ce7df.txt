Chancellor Jeremy Hunt has responded to the rate of inflation which remains above 10%, according to official figures showing record costs for some essential foods.

He assured that the “UK is on the right track to focus on bringing down inflation”, with Rishi Sunak aiming to half inflation by the end of 2023, despite “a large rise in food inflation which is causing pressure to families”.
