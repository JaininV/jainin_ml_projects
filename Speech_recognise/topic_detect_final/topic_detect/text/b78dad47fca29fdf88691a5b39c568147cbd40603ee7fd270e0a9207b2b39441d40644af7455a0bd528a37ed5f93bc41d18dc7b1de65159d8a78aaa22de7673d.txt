Warner Bros. has announced a new Harry Potter game to follow up the success of Hogwarts Legacy. Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions is in active development and the company is seeking players to take part in playtests to detect any bugs or design flaws. Warner Bros says the game is a “fast-paced, competitive multiplayer game featuring the world’s most iconic magical sport.” It comes as Hogwarts Legacy has generated more than £685m ($850m) in sales.
