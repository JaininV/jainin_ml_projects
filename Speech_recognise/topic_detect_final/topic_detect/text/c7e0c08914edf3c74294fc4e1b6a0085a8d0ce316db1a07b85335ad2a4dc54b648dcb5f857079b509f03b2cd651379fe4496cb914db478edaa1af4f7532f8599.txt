,  /PRNewswire/ -- Golden Grail Technology doing business as Golden Grail Beverages, (OTC: GOGY), is pleased to announce our expanded partnership with Southeastern Marketing & Distribution to include distribution of Sway Energy in the state of Kentucky.

Southeastern Marketing & Distribution is a premier wholesale distributor of convenience store products and general merchandise offering a large variety of energy drinks, dietary herbal supplements, medicines, pain relievers, health and personal care items, cell phone accessories and more.

"Southeastern Marketing & Distribution is our distributor for Cause Water in the state of Kentucky and we are happy to announce that they will also be our distributor for Sway Energy in Kentucky" said Steve Hoffman, CEO of Golden Grail.
