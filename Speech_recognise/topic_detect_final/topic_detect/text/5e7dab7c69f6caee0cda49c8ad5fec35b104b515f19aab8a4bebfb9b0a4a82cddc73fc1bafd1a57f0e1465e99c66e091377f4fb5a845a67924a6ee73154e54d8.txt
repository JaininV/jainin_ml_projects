There are 49 people waiting on hospital beds at the North West’s two main Hospitals today.

The figures were released as part of the INMO’s Trolley watch figures.

There are 29 people waiting on a hospital bed at Sligo university Hospital while the number in Letterkenny is 20.

There are 19 and 5 people waiting at the respective Emergency Departments.

Nationwide there are 562 people waiting on beds across the Country.
