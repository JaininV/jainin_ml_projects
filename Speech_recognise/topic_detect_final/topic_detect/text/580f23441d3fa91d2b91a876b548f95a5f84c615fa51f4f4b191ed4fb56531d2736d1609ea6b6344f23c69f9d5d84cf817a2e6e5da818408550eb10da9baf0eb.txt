Clifford Allen Marsh, 81, Wabash, died at 8:25 a.m. Tuesday, April 11, 2023, at his home.

He was born July 27, 1941. On Feb. 27, 1965, he married Shirley Ann Blessing, who preceded him in death in 2015.

He is survived by two children, Robert (Connie) Marsh, Columbia City, and Sandra Marsh, Wabash; and three grandchildren.
