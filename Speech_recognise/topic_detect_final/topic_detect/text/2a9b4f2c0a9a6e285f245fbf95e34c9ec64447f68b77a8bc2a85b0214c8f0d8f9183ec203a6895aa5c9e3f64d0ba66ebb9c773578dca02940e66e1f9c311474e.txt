1. Choose a

ripe dragon fruit. Look for one that has a

bright pink or red colour and feels soft to the touch.

2. Cut the dragon fruit in half and scoop out the flesh. Discard the skin and seeds.

3. Mash the dragon fruit flesh with a

fork until it forms a

paste.

4. Apply the paste to your face and let it sit for 15 to 20 minutes.

5. Rinse your face with lukewarm water, then pat it dry with a

clean towel.

6. Apply a

moisturiser or natural oil to your face after using the dragon fruit paste.
