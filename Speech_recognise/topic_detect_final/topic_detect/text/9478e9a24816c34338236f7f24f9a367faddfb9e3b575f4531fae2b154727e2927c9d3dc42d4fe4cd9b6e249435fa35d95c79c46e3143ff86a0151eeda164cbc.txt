A major event is set to take place in Sligo to mark the anniversary of Natalia Karaczyn  who murdered by her husband in 2018

A charity event in aid of domestic violence charity DIVAS will be taking place in Sligo on April 29th.

The fundraiser will take place at the GAA pitch in Cleveragh with a number of different activities taking place on the day.

Natalia’s sister, Magdalena McMorrow, is one of the main organisers of the event.

Speaking to reporter Claire Ronan, she outlined what exactly will be happening on the day and encouraged people to donate.
