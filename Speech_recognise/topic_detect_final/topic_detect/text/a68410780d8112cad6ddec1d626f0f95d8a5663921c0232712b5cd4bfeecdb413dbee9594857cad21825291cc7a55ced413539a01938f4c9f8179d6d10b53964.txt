The Earthen Plasters Industry is a sustainable construction industry that uses natural materials such as clay, sand, and straw to create plasters and coatings for walls, ceilings, and floors. This industry is gaining popularity due to the growing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable construction materials.

The earthen plasters market size is expected to grow from USD 86 million in 2021 to USD 103 million by 2026, at a CAGR of 3.5% during the forecast period. The demand for earthen plasters is expected to increase in the years 2023-24 due to the growing awareness of environmental issues and the need for sustainable construction practices. Additionally, governments around the world are implementing regulations and policies to encourage the use of sustainable construction materials, which is also driving the demand for earthen plasters.

Earthen plasters have several benefits over traditional plasters, such as their thermal and acoustic insulation properties, their ability to regulate humidity and temperature, and their natural beauty. They also require less energy and resources to manufacture and have a lower carbon footprint compared to traditional plasters.

The scope of the earthen plasters industry is vast, and it includes not only the production and distribution of earthen plasters but also the training and education of architects, builders, and homeowners on how to use and apply earthen plasters. This industry also provides employment opportunities for individuals interested in sustainable construction practices and natural building techniques.
