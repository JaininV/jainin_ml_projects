Check out the official trailer for the new action thriller "Assassin Club," starring Henry Golding (from "Crazy Rich Asians" and "Snake Eyes") as a professional hitman who finds himself in the crosshairs of several other assassins who are just as skilled as he is.

Directed by French action director Camille Delamarre (The Transporter Refueled, Brick Mansions), the action film, which appears to be riding the wave of John Wick's success, also stars Daniela Melchior (The Suicide Squad) and Jimmy-Jean Louis (Claws) with Noomi Rapace (Lamb, Prometheus) and Sam Neill (Jurassic Park).

"Assassin Club" is slated to be released on Digital on May 16th before arriving on Blu-ray and DVD on June 6th. Watch the trailer, above.

synopsis:

Henry Golding (G. I. JOE: SNAKE EYES) stars in this highly entertaining, globe-trotting action thriller. The hunter becomes the hunted when an elite assassin (Golding) is given his final contract: to kill seven people around the world -- only to discover the targets are equally skilled assassins hired to kill him. His only shot to survive is to uncover the mysterious mastermind behind the deadly plot before it's too late.
