Ese Awusa has clocked a new Personal Best (PB) of 13.40 seconds (1.2) to win the women’s 100 meters hurdle at the 2023 Jo Meaker Classic in Texas.

The improved national standard is No. 1 in the NJCAA. The performance earned the sophomore the NJCAA women’s division USTFCCCA National Athlete of the Week honors for the first time in her career.
